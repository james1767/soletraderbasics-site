# Xero vs FreeAgent for Sole Traders: Which Should You Pick?

**Most sole traders should choose [FreeAgent](#).** It’s simpler, built specifically for UK freelancers, and can file your Self Assessment directly to HMRC.

Choose [Xero](#) if you plan to grow, hire staff, or need advanced features and integrations.

## 🥇 Why FreeAgent is better for most sole traders

**Built for UK sole traders**

FreeAgent is designed specifically for freelancers and sole traders. Everything is focused on what you actually need: tracking income, expenses, and tax.

**It files your Self Assessment**

👉 You can submit your tax return directly to HMRC from within [FreeAgent](#)

This removes the need for:

- separate software
- manual calculations
- extra admin

**Real-time tax visibility**

FreeAgent shows a running estimate of:

- Income Tax
- National Insurance
- payments on account

👉 You always know roughly what you owe.

**Simple to use**

You don’t need accounting knowledge.

The interface is:

- guided
- clearly labelled
- designed for non-accountants
