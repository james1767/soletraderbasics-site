## What accounting software actually does

Modern accounting software replaces spreadsheets and manual tracking.

It will:

- connect to your bank account
- automatically import transactions
- categorise income and expenses
- estimate your tax bill
- generate reports
- help you file your tax return

👉 You don’t need to be “good with numbers” — the software does most of the work.

## Do you need an accountant?

Not always.

Many sole traders use software like FreeAgent and file their own tax return.

You may still want an accountant if:

- your income is high
- your situation is complex
- you want advice on tax efficiency
