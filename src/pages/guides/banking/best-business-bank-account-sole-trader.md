## Do sole traders need a business bank account?

Not legally — but in practice, yes.

Using a separate account makes it much easier to:

- track income and expenses
- stay organised for tax
- avoid mixing personal and business spending
- work with accounting software

👉 Most sole traders open a business account early to keep things simple.

## Free vs paid accounts

Most sole traders do **not** need a paid account.

Free accounts like Mettle, Starling, and Tide cover:

- receiving payments
- sending payments
- basic expense tracking
- integration with accounting software

You may consider paid plans later if you need:

- multiple users
- advanced tools
- higher limits
