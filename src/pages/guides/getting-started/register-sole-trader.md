# How to Register as a Sole Trader UK

## Quick answer

To register as a sole trader in the UK, you need to sign up for Self Assessment with HMRC.

👉 You can register here: https://www.gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment

The process takes about 10–15 minutes.

---

## When do you need to register?

You must register if:

- you earn more than £1,000 from self-employment in a tax year
- you regularly sell goods or services
- you intend to run a business

This £1,000 threshold is called the **trading allowance**.

👉 If you earn less than this, you may not need to register.

---

## Step-by-step: how to register

### 1. Go to HMRC

Visit:

https://www.gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment

---

### 2. Create or sign in to your Government Gateway account

You’ll either:

- create a new account
- or log in if you already have one

---

### 3. Fill in your details

You’ll need:

- your name and address
- National Insurance number
- date you started your business

---

### 4. Choose “Sole Trader”

Make sure you select:

```text
Self-employed / Sole Trader
```
